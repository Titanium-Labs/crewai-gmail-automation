üìä Detailed Data Processing Information

üîÑ How Your Email Data Flows Through Our System

Step 1: Data Collection
  • We fetch unread emails from your Gmail inbox
  • We read email content, headers, and metadata
  • We access your existing Gmail labels and settings
Step 2: AI Processing
  • Email content is sent to OpenAI's API for analysis
  • AI categorizes emails by type (Important, Newsletter, Receipt, etc.)
  • AI determines priority levels (High, Medium, Low)
  • AI generates draft responses for important emails
Step 3: Gmail Modifications
  • We apply appropriate labels to your emails
  • We star high-priority emails
  • We delete low-priority emails based on age rules
  • We create draft responses in your Gmail drafts folder
